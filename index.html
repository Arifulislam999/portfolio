An effective website design should be both engaging and intuitive. Creating a great user experience is essential, to do so your website needs to be well optimised for usability and functional. This may sound easy enough but in reality it takes an experienced designer to marry all these factors together.

RESPONSIVE DESIGN

Responsively designed sites that leave our workshop, will automatically adapt to fit the screen it’s being viewed on, providing a seamless experience regardless of the device you are viewing from. Not only is this great for SEO, customers love the ease with which they can navigate the site and find the information they’re looking for and take their shopping sessions from one device to the next. Having a responsively designed website will open a whole new avenue of mobile and tablet customers, offer a better user experience and most importantly give the right first impression.
